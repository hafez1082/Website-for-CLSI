Fostering Excellence in Laboratory Medicine

CLSI brings together the worldwide laboratory community to advance a common cause.

Our Vision

Setting the standard in laboratory medicine for a healthier world.

Our Mission

Develop clinical and laboratory practices and promote their use worldwide.

Our Values

  • Inclusiveness

    • We include the viewpoints of industry, government, and the health care professions in a consensus-driven process.


  • Excellence

    • We continuously improve upon our tradition of technical excellence and superior quality.


  • Responsiveness

    • We proactively identify and respond to the needs of our stakeholders in an open and timely manner.


  • Integrity

    • We act ethically and with fairness, trust, respect, and openness.


  • Teamwork

    • We are committed to effective collaboration among members, volunteers, staff, and other partners.
