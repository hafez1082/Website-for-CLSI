About the CLSI Standards Development Process

CLSI’s unique consensus process brings together experts from industry, government, and health care professions to develop timely standards recognized by laboratories, industry, accreditors, and regulators as the way to improve testing.

About Our Standards & Guidelines

CLSI documents focus on a variety of specialty areas, from laboratory basics to quality management systems, verification and validation , and information management. We also develop interactive tools, companion products, and bench aids based on these documents.
