About CLSI

The Global Leader in Setting Clinical Laboratory Standards

The Clinical and Laboratory Standards Institute (CLSI) is a not-for-profit organization that develops laboratory standards worldwide. Our standards are recognized by laboratories, accreditors, and government agencies as the best way to improve medical laboratory testing.

CLSI by numbers

24,000+

Countries with CLSI members.

We improve patient care by bringing experts together to develop standards. We facilitate the creation of best practice standards, guidelines, and products for medical laboratories by using the expertise of our volunteers. Organizations use CLSI standards to improve their testing outcomes, maintain accreditation, bring products to market faster, and navigate regulatory hurdles.
